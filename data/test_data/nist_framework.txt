NIST CYBERSECURITY FRAMEWORK - POLICY TEMPLATE GUIDE
CIS MS-ISAC Reference Standards (2024)

==================================================
CORE FUNCTIONS AND CATEGORIES
==================================================

1. IDENTIFY (ID)
   - Asset Management (ID.AM)
   - Business Environment (ID.BE)
   - Governance (ID.GV)
   - Risk Assessment (ID.RA)
   - Risk Management Strategy (ID.RM)
   - Supply Chain Risk Management (ID.SC)

2. PROTECT (PR)
   - Identity Management and Access Control (PR.AC)
   - Awareness and Training (PR.AT)
   - Data Security (PR.DS)
   - Information Protection Processes and Procedures (PR.IP)
   - Maintenance (PR.MA)
   - Protective Technology (PR.PT)

3. DETECT (DE)
   - Anomalies and Events (DE.AE)
   - Security Continuous Monitoring (DE.CM)
   - Detection Processes (DE.DP)

4. RESPOND (RS)
   - Response Planning (RS.RP)
   - Communications (RS.CO)
   - Analysis (RS.AN)
   - Mitigation (RS.MI)
   - Improvements (RS.IM)

5. RECOVER (RC)
   - Recovery Planning (RC.RP)
   - Improvements (RC.IM)
   - Communications (RC.CO)

==================================================
INFORMATION SECURITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (ISMS)
==================================================

Required Elements:
- Information security policy statement and objectives
- Scope of ISMS (boundaries and applicability)
- Roles and responsibilities for information security
- Risk assessment methodology and criteria
- Risk treatment process and acceptance criteria
- Statement of applicability (SOA)
- Internal audit procedures
- Management review process
- Continual improvement mechanisms
- Documentation and record keeping requirements
- Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

==================================================
DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY
==================================================

Required Elements:
- Data classification scheme (public, internal, confidential, restricted)
- Data collection, processing, and retention policies
- Consent management and user rights
- Data encryption requirements (at rest and in transit)
- Access control mechanisms and authentication
- Data breach notification procedures
- Privacy impact assessments
- Third-party data sharing agreements
- Data disposal and destruction procedures
- Cross-border data transfer controls
- Privacy by design principles
- Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations

==================================================
PATCH MANAGEMENT
==================================================

Required Elements:
- Patch management policy scope and objectives
- Roles and responsibilities for patch management
- Asset inventory and criticality classification
- Vulnerability assessment procedures
- Patch testing and validation process
- Patch deployment schedule and prioritization
- Emergency patch procedures for critical vulnerabilities
- Rollback procedures for failed patches
- Patch compliance monitoring and reporting
- Vendor notification and patch source verification
- Change management integration
- Documentation and audit trail requirements

==================================================
RISK MANAGEMENT
==================================================

Required Elements:
- Risk management framework and methodology
- Risk identification process
- Risk analysis and evaluation criteria
- Risk appetite and tolerance levels
- Risk treatment options (accept, mitigate, transfer, avoid)
- Risk register and documentation
- Risk monitoring and review procedures
- Roles and responsibilities for risk management
- Integration with business continuity planning
- Third-party and supply chain risk assessment
- Incident response and risk correlation
- Risk reporting to senior management and board
- Continuous risk assessment process

==================================================
KEY SECURITY CONTROLS
==================================================

Access Control:
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for privileged accounts
- Least privilege principle
- Regular access reviews and recertification
- Segregation of duties

Incident Response:
- Incident response plan and procedures
- Incident classification and severity levels
- Communication protocols and escalation paths
- Post-incident review and lessons learned

Business Continuity:
- Business impact analysis (BIA)
- Recovery time objectives (RTO) and recovery point objectives (RPO)
- Backup and restoration procedures
- Disaster recovery testing

Security Awareness:
- Regular security training for all employees
- Phishing simulation exercises
- Acceptable use policy
- Reporting procedures for security incidents

Monitoring and Logging:
- Centralized log management
- Security information and event management (SIEM)
- Log retention policies
- Real-time alerting for security events

==================================================
COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

- Regular security audits and assessments
- Penetration testing and vulnerability scanning
- Compliance with industry standards (ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI DSS)
- Regulatory compliance reporting
- Third-party security assessments
- Security metrics and KPIs
- Executive reporting and governance
